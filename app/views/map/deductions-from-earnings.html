Deductions from earnings

If you need to pay a penalty, would you like us to deduct it from yoru earnings?

The court may deduct a penalty from your earnings if:

  • you've failed to pay in the past
  • you're ordered to pay compensation, as part of your penalty

For guideance on amount of deduction please visit:

http://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2006/501/regulation/5/made
