To get the most out of this demo use the details below on the "Find your case" screen.

Case or unique reference number

One of the following must be used:

  • Transport for London: TFL, TfL, tfl or 21NT5181416
  • TV Licensing: TVL, tvl, 123123123C or 506123456C
  • Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency: DVLA, dvla or 29K/WN02ZRR

Postcodes

  • any postcode can be used, however, for continuity use: W1 7SX or W17SX
  • AN9 9YZ or AN99YZ can also be used
  • to see the Welsh language questions in the not guilty flow: LL48 6ER or LL486ER

Timeout

  • An overlay will be displayed after 60 seconds of inactivity
  • Session will timeout after 90 seconds
